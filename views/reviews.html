Located on the river, Chon Mon Restaurant & Bar is rapidly becoming one of Hoi An's great casual dining establishments. With classical views of the old quarters of Hoi An, Chon Mon offers guests great restaurant quality Vietnamese street food and local dishes, as well as home cooked western food options and children menus. Why not enjoy our casual setting with a cold beer or freshly made cocktails. Just sit back, enjoy the view and relax, Chon Mon is what Vietnam is all about!
