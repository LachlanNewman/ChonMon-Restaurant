Cooking Classes

Welcome to Chon Mon Alacarte we offer cooking classes for all those interested in knowing the secrets of Vietnamese cuisine. Immerse yourself in the fascinating world of Vietnamese food with expert guidance and advice from a Master Chef.

This 4-hour class will leave your taste buds tingling as you mix and match Vietnamese ingredients to cook and eat stunning food from one of the most delicious Asian cuisines! In our cooking classes, we offer a hands-on approach. Participants are guided through all the steps with the end result of a typical Vietnamese menu, which not only tastes delicious, but is also a visual joy.

Imagine how you could surprise your dinner guests next time with a perfect Vietnamese meal! Learn to prepare spring rolls, soups, seafood dishes or Vietnamese signature dishes guided by our professional English speaking chef.

Join our chef in a tour of local Hoi An food market to choose the ingredients for the cooking class! This is a chance to really discover what the food of Vietnam is all about. Be mesmerized by the smells, the textures and the colours that are so distinctive to this part of the world.

Then sit down and enjoy your own creations! In our Restaurants private dinning area, enjoying the views of old Hoi An.

And when you leave, receive a copy of the recipes and a Vietnamese cooking gift to take home.
